This data is from MLB games from 1903-2021, including postseason games. Data are complete back to 1973, mostly complete back to 1950, and somewhat complete back to 1916. It omits games that are less than 9 innings (doubleheaders in 2020) as well as extra inning games where a runner starts on second base (non-playoff games in 2020 and 2021). All data is available at Retrosheet.

Linked from: FiveThirtyEight (search for "eighth inning"), The Washington Post, CBS Sports, CBS Sports again, The Athletic, Royals Review, Beyond the Box Score, Talking Chop, and more!
